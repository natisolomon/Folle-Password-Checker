About Password Checker

Password Checker is a simple web application that helps you evaluate the strength of your passwords. It analyzes your password based on various criteria such as length, complexity, and character types to provide you with an indication of its strength.

This project is developed using HTML, CSS, and possibly JavaScript for any interactive features. It aims to provide users with a quick and easy way to assess the security of their passwords and make informed decisions to improve their online security.

Why is a Password Strength Checker

A weak password can be easily hacked, leaving you vulnerable to
identity theft, fraud, and other cybercrimes. That's why it's essential
to understand the importance of password strength and how to create a secure
password that can't be cracked.

Why use a good password?

Strong passwords are of the utmost importance. They protect your
electronic accounts and devices from unauthorized access, keeping your
sensitive personal information safe. The more complex the password, the
more protected your information will be from cyber threats and hackers.
